Unwrap me
the way songs do to my emotions,
uncovering the raw side of mine
Let my parts tumble and spreed out over the mud
The way rose petals do,
feeling yet learning
Drown me in My tears
So that you are not to take the flame
Hug my screams
The way you hands round my face,
comforing while also
weakening my heart yet making me strong 
Take me under the star
And let my words complete in your lap
As i am incomplete as this poetry
And need you to complete me with your moons and stars
