She left my oyester, months ago
Yet she converted my dirt to pearl
She left my life, months ago
Yet she is here, in the walls
In the scribbles of my paper,
In the broken glasses all over my room 
In the ramp, we used to walk on
In the marigoldin my yard
In the mirror and my eyes
In my tears and my smile
In the ignored letters she wrote to me
In her poetries whose muse was I....
